## FAQ — Contact specific

**Will I be speaking to Jimmy directly?**
Yes. Maru is a solo practice. Jimmy handles every engagement personally — from the first conversation through to handover. There is no team behind the scenes managing your account.

**What should I prepare before the 20-minute call?**
Nothing formal. It helps to have a rough sense of your biggest operational frustration and what you've already tried to fix it — but even that isn't required. The call is a conversation, not a presentation.

**I'm not based in Johannesburg — can we still work together?**
Yes. The diagnostic and most engagement work is handled remotely. The intake form, verification call, and all delivery happens via video and shared documents. For Gauteng-based clients we can meet in person at key stages if that's useful.

**How quickly can we get started?**
The diagnostic can begin as soon as the intake form is completed and payment is confirmed — typically within 24 hours of your booking. Availability for the core engagement depends on current client load. We'll be transparent about timeline during the scoping conversation.
