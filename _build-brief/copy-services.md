## Section — What we build
### Heading: What a Maru engagement actually looks like

Every engagement follows the same four-phase process. The scope is fixed before we start. The price is fixed before we start. Nothing gets added to your invoice mid-project.

**Phase 1 — Diagnose**
We start with a structured diagnostic. You complete a sector-specific intake form — fifteen questions, designed for mobile, takes about fifteen minutes. We follow up with a short verification call. Within 48 hours you receive a diagnostic report that maps your current gaps, quantifies what they're costing you in Rands, and outlines the three highest-impact interventions for your business.

The diagnostic costs R4,500. If you proceed to a full engagement, that fee offsets against the project cost.

**Phase 2 — Design**
We take the diagnostic findings and build a fixed-scope implementation plan. You know exactly what we're building, in what sequence, and what it will cost before you commit to anything.

**Phase 3 — Build**
We build. If your site or digital infrastructure needs remediation before automation can work effectively, that's sprint one — scoped and priced upfront, not discovered halfway through. We fix the foundation first.

**Phase 4 — Launch and measure**
Every engagement includes a 30-day measurement phase. We set a baseline before we build, track what changes after launch, and deliver a results report that shows you exactly what moved and by how much. This phase is built into every engagement — not an optional add-on.
