## The four phases
### Heading: The engagement — phase by phase

---

### Phase 1 — Diagnose

**The work:** You complete a sector-specific intake form — fifteen questions, designed for mobile, takes about fifteen minutes. We follow up with a short verification call. Within 48 hours you receive your diagnostic report.

**What the diagnostic covers:**
Your workflow and process audit — where manual effort is highest and where automation would have the most impact. Your AI tool inventory — what you're currently running, how it's connected, and where the gaps are. Your site infrastructure assessment — speed, mobile performance, SEO basics, and any technical issues that would undermine an automation layer built on top. And a revenue gap analysis — specific to your numbers, quantified in Rands.

**What you receive:** A five-section written report delivered as a live document. Business snapshot, what's working, cost of current state, priority map, and recommended next step. The report is specific to your business — not adapted from a template.

**Investment: R4,500 — offsets against the core engagement if you proceed.**

---

### Phase 2 — Design

**The work:** We take the diagnostic findings and build a fixed-scope implementation plan. Every item is specified — what we're building, in what sequence, what it connects to, and what it's designed to produce. Nothing is vague.

**What you receive:** A fixed-scope proposal with a single fixed price. You review it, ask questions, request adjustments. We don't proceed until you've signed off on every element.

**The gate:** If the diagnostic surfaces a critical site infrastructure issue — a broken foundation that would undermine anything built on top of it — the implementation plan addresses that first. Sprint one is always the foundation. We tell you this upfront, not halfway through the build.

---

### Phase 3 — Build

**The work:** We build what the plan specifies. If site remediation is sprint one, that goes first. The automation layer follows once the foundation is sound. Every sprint has a defined output and a defined completion point.

**What you receive:** Working systems, not prototypes. Everything built is tested, documented, and handed over with instructions your team can follow without a technical background. We build for independence, not dependency.

---

### Phase 4 — Launch and measure

**The work:** Before we build anything, we set a baseline — the numbers that matter to your business right now. After launch we track what changes over 30 days. At the end of the measurement period you receive a results report.

**What you receive:** A clear before-and-after picture. Not "the system is live" — "here's what moved, here's what didn't, here's what to watch." This phase is built into every engagement. It is not optional and it is not an add-on.

**Why this matters:** The measurement phase is where your case study gets built. It's also where the decision about Phase 5 gets made — based on data, not on a sales conversation.

---

### Phase 5 — Optimise *(optional)*

**The work:** A second fixed-scope engagement triggered by what Phase 4 surfaces. Not a retainer. Not an open-ended monthly commitment. A defined 60-day sprint built around the specific opportunities the measurement phase identified.

**When it makes sense:** When Phase 4 shows clear results and an obvious next layer of improvement. When the business has stabilised on the first engagement and is ready to build further. When the data makes the case — not when we decide it's time to sell you something else.

**Investment: R15,000–R25,000 depending on scope. Discussed after Phase 4 handover, never before.**

## Operating principles
### Heading: What you can expect from every engagement

**Fixed scope before we start.** You know exactly what we're building before you commit to anything. No "we'll figure it out as we go."

**Fixed price before we start.** One number, agreed upfront. No hourly billing, no scope additions, no invoice surprises.

**Foundation first.** If your site or digital infrastructure is broken, we fix it before we build on it. We will tell you this clearly — even if it means the engagement takes longer or costs more than you initially expected.

**Independence at handover.** Every system we build is documented and handed over so your team can run it without us. If it only works when we're involved, we haven't done our job.

**Honest measurement.** The 30-day results report tells you what actually changed — including what didn't work as expected. We don't curate results to protect the relationship. We report what the data shows and tell you what it means.

**Maximum five clients at any time.** This isn't a marketing line. It's how we ensure every engagement gets full attention. When you work with Maru you work with Jimmy — not an account manager or a junior analyst.

## FAQ — Embedded

**How long does the whole process take?**
The diagnostic takes 48 hours from intake form submission to report delivery. The core engagement — Phases 2 through 4 — typically runs six to ten weeks depending on complexity and whether site remediation is required. The 30-day measurement phase runs after launch. Phase 5, if you choose it, is a separate 60-day sprint.

**Do I need to be technical to work with you?**
No. We build systems your team can use and maintain without a technical background. Everything is documented in plain language at handover. If something breaks after we've handed over, we're reachable — but the systems are designed not to need us.

**What if my business isn't ready for AI implementation?**
The diagnostic will tell you. If the honest answer is that your foundation needs work before AI automation makes sense, we'll say so — and we can scope a site remediation engagement at R8,000–R15,000 to get you there. We'd rather give you a clear picture than sell you something you're not ready for.

**I already have AI tools. Do I have to replace them?**
Almost certainly not. Our first obligation is to audit what you have and make it work better. We only recommend new tools when there is a genuine capability gap your existing stack cannot fill — and we explain exactly why when that happens.

**How many clients do you work with at once?**
Maximum five. That's a hard limit, not a soft guideline. It's how we protect the quality of every engagement.

**What happens if the results don't meet expectations?**
The 30-day measurement phase is where this gets addressed honestly. If something didn't perform as expected, the results report says so and explains why. We don't disappear after handover. The 30-day check-in is built in specifically to catch this and course-correct where needed.

**Can I start with just the diagnostic and decide later?**
Yes — that's exactly how it's designed. The diagnostic is a complete, standalone deliverable. There is no obligation to proceed to a full engagement. Many clients use the diagnostic report to make an internal case for the investment before committing.

**Do you work outside Johannesburg?**
The diagnostic and most of the engagement work is handled remotely. For clients in Gauteng we can meet in person at key stages. For clients elsewhere in South Africa the process works entirely via video call and shared documents — same quality, same process.
