## Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

**Voice Characteristics:**
- **Confident but not arrogant:** We know our expertise, but we respect yours
- **Direct but not blunt:** Clear communication without being harsh
- **Empathetic but not soft:** We understand your concerns without being overly emotional
- **Technical but not jargon-heavy:** We explain complexity in accessible terms

**Tone Variations by Section:**
- **Hero:** Bold, promise-driven, immediately valuable
- **Problem:** Empathetic, understanding, validating
- **Benefits:** Confident, results-focused, specific
- **Services:** Professional, detailed, transparent
- **Testimonials:** Authentic, relatable, metric-driven
- **Lead Magnet:** Helpful, low-pressure, value-first

---

### Writing Principles

**1. Lead with outcomes, not features**
❌ "We use advanced AI configuration techniques"  
✅ "Your chatbot sounds like your team, not a robot"

**2. Be specific with metrics**
❌ "Significant improvement in conversion rates"  
✅ "187% increase in lead conversion within 90 days"

**3. Address both practical and emotional needs**
❌ "We implement AI systems"  
✅ "We implement AI that drives ROI while preserving authentic relationships"

**4. Use "you" language, not "we" language**
❌ "We provide implementation services"  
✅ "You get step-by-step playbooks tailored to your business"

**5. Make the complex accessible**
❌ "Synergistic RevOps orchestration leveraging AI-native paradigms"  
✅ "We connect your marketing, sales, and customer success systems so leads actually convert"

## Mobile Responsiveness Notes

### Hero Section
- Stack headline and subheadline vertically
- Reduce headline font size to 36px
- Stack CTAs vertically with full width
- Simplify split-screen visual to single dashboard with before/after toggle

### Problem Section
- Stack statistics vertically instead of horizontally
- Reduce data visualization complexity for smaller screens
- Maintain readability of body copy at 16px minimum

- Single column layout (cards stack vertically)
- Maintain icon size and visual hierarchy
- Ensure touch targets are minimum 44px

### Services Section
- Accordion/expandable cards work well on mobile
- One service visible at a time
- Maintain readability of detailed descriptions

### Testimonials
- Single column carousel with swipe gestures
- Maintain quote readability
- Stack metrics vertically within each card

- Full-width form fields
- Stack form and benefits copy vertically
- Maintain button visibility and touch target size
