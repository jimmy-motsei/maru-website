## Introduction

The Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) became fully enforceable in July 2021, yet many South African businesses continue to implement AI solutions without fully understanding their compliance obligations.

This isn't about fear—it's about building trust. When your customers know their data is handled responsibly, they're more likely to engage with your AI-powered services.

**This checklist will help you:**
- Audit your current AI implementations for POPIA compliance
- Identify gaps before they become costly problems
- Build customer trust through transparent data practices
- Avoid penalties of up to R10 million or imprisonment

---

## Part 1: Data Collection Compliance

### ✅ Checklist

- [ ] **Lawful Purpose Defined**
  - Have you documented the specific, legitimate purpose for collecting each data point?
  - Is this purpose communicated clearly to data subjects?

- [ ] **Consent Mechanism Active**
  - Do you have explicit opt-in consent for AI processing (not pre-ticked boxes)?
  - Can users easily withdraw consent at any time?

- [ ] **Minimum Data Principle**
  - Are you only collecting data that is strictly necessary for the stated purpose?
  - Have you audited AI tools that may collect additional data automatically?

- [ ] **Privacy Notice Updated**
  - Does your privacy policy specifically mention AI/automated processing?
  - Have you explained what AI decisions are made about users?

- [ ] **Children's Data Protected**
  - If processing data of persons under 18, do you have guardian consent?
  - Are AI systems configured to handle minors' data appropriately?

---

## Part 2: AI Processing & Automated Decisions

### ✅ Checklist

- [ ] **Transparency on Automated Decisions**
  - Do customers know when they're interacting with AI (chatbots, recommendations)?
  - Can users request human review of AI-made decisions?

- [ ] **Profiling Disclosure**
  - If AI creates customer profiles or segments, is this disclosed?
  - Have you documented the logic behind profiling algorithms?

- [ ] **No Discriminatory Outcomes**
  - Have you tested AI systems for bias across demographic groups?
  - Is there a process to address discriminatory AI outputs?

- [ ] **Data Accuracy Maintained**
  - How often is training data reviewed for accuracy?
  - Can data subjects correct inaccurate information used by AI?

- [ ] **Security Measures Implemented**
  - Is AI-processed data encrypted in transit and at rest?
  - Are access controls in place for AI systems?

- [ ] **Retention Limits Defined**
  - How long does AI retain personal data for processing?
  - Is there automatic deletion after the retention period?

- [ ] **Cross-Border Transfers Compliant**
  - If AI data is processed outside South Africa, is this disclosed?
  - Do you have adequate safeguards for international transfers?

## Part 3: Third-Party AI Tools

### ✅ Checklist

| Tool Type | Compliance Question |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)** | Is the data processing agreement POPIA-compliant? |
| **AI Chatbots (ChatGPT, Intercom)** | Where is conversation data stored and processed? |
| **Email Automation (Mailchimp, Klaviyo)** | Are unsubscribe mechanisms functioning correctly? |
| **Analytics (Google Analytics 4)** | Have you configured privacy-compliant settings? |
| **AI Writing Tools (Jasper, Copy.ai)** | Is customer data inputted into these tools? |

### Key Questions for Vendors:

1. Where is data physically stored?
2. Who has access to the data?
3. How long is data retained?
4. What happens to data if the contract ends?
5. Can they demonstrate POPIA compliance?

## Part 4: Customer Communications

### ✅ AI Chatbots

- [ ] Users know they're speaking to a bot (clear disclosure)
- [ ] Escalation to human agent is always available
- [ ] Chat transcripts are stored securely with retention limits
- [ ] Personal data from chats isn't used for training without consent

### ✅ Email Automation

- [ ] Clear sender identification (not misleading)
- [ ] Easy unsubscribe in every email
- [ ] Preference centres allow granular control
- [ ] AI-generated content is reviewed for accuracy

### ✅ Personalization

- [ ] Users can opt out of AI recommendations
- [ ] Personalization logic is explainable
- [ ] Sensitive categories aren't used for targeting without consent

## Part 5: Data Subject Rights

Under POPIA, individuals have the right to:

| Right | Your AI System Must... |
|-------|------------------------|
| **Access** | Provide data held about them, including AI-derived insights |
| **Correction** | Allow updates to inaccurate data used by AI |
| **Deletion** | Remove personal data from AI systems on request |
| **Objection** | Stop AI processing for direct marketing |
| **Portability** | Export data in a usable format |

### ✅ Checklist

- [ ] Process exists to handle data subject requests within 30 days
- [ ] AI systems can identify and extract individual data
- [ ] Deletion requests propagate to all AI subsystems
- [ ] Staff trained on handling rights requests
